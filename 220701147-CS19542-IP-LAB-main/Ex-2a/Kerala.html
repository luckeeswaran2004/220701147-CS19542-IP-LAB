KERALA

Kerala, a state located in the southwestern part of India, is known for its natural beauty, rich cultural heritage, and vibrant traditions./

  • Language: Malayalam is the official language of Kerala.
    It has a rich literary tradition, with classical and modern literature that reflects the state's diverse cultural influences.
  • Literature: Kerala’s literature includes classical works such as the Ramacharitam and Thunchath Ezhuthachan’s Adhyatma Ramayanam.
    Modern literature features prominent figures like Vaikom Muhammad Basheer, O. V. Vijayan, and M. T. Vasudevan Nair.
